We are passionate about providing “decision footholds” that serve as mechanisms for informational decision-making in educational settings. Our organization helps youngsters to effectively increase the confidence that they made the right career choice. To decrease the uncertainty, we have developed a career decision-making mobile app that delivers research-based advice. The app informs about the optimal steps towards a career planning consistent with personal objectives and seemly hidden future obstacles.
